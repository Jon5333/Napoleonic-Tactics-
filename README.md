# Napoleonic Tactics: Pixel Edition

A turn-based tactical strategy game built with Vanilla JavaScript and HTML5 Canvas. 

## ‚öîÔ∏è Key Features
- **Unit Drafting:** Build your army from Infantry, Cavalry, Artillery, and Officers.
- **Formations:** Toggle between **Line** (standard), **Column** (speed), and **Square** (anti-cav).
- **Tactical Depth:** - **Officer Auras:** Units within 2 tiles of an officer get a 25% damage boost.
  - **Unit Facing:** Attacking from the flank or rear deals 1.5x - 2.0x damage.
  - **Terrain:** Forests and Hills provide defense but slow down movement.
- **Aggressive AI:** An enemy commander that hunts your units and utilizes flanking.

## üöÄ How to Play
1. **Draft:** Select 5 units for your regiment.
2. **Deploy:** Click on the bottom two rows to place your troops.
3. **Move:** Click a unit, then click a highlighted tile to move (Movement is locked once used).
4. **Attack:** Click your unit, then click an adjacent enemy.
5. **Form:** Change formations to adapt to the battlefield.
